Getting started

Starting therapy is simpler than you think.

No paperwork surprise, no complicated intake forms before you even know if we're the right fit. Just three straightforward steps.

01

Request your free consult

Fill out the form on this page or call us at (732) 201-6325. We'll reach back within 24 hours — usually the same day. No obligation, no credit card, nothing to prepare.

02

15-minute phone call

A friendly, no-pressure conversation. We ask what's bringing you to therapy, answer your questions, and match you with the therapist best suited to your situation. If we're not the right fit, we'll point you somewhere better.

03

Your first session — this week

Meet your therapist in person at our Rutherford office or via secure video — whichever fits your life. Sessions run 45–55 minutes. Most clients notice meaningful progress within the first few sessions.
